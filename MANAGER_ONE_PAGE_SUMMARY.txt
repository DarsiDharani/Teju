## ğŸ’¡ The Three Components (What Gets Measured)

### 1. TRAINING ATTENDANCE (30% weight)
- Did they show up? **YES = 100%, NO = 0%**
- *Why?* Can't learn if they don't attend

### 2. ASSIGNMENT SCORES (40% weight)
- What do their quizzes/assignments show? **Average of all scores (0-100)**
- *Why?* Direct measure of knowledge and understanding (MOST IMPORTANT)

### 3. MANAGER FEEDBACK (30% weight)
- How does the manager rate their performance? **Average rating 1-5 converted to 0-100%**
- *Why?* Shows if they can actually apply learning in real work
